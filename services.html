Neurology

Brain & Nervous System Care

VitalCare runs Lagos's first dedicated private Stroke Unit with hyperacute thrombolysis capability. Our neurologists manage the full spectrum of neurological conditions with 3T MRI and EEG suites on-site.

We run specialist clinics for epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, dementia, and headache disorders, supported by a multidisciplinary team.
