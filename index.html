About VitalCare

🏥

2004 Founded
Our Story

More Than a Hospital —
A Health Community

VitalCare was founded in 2004 by a small group of physicians who shared a simple but powerful belief: every Nigerian deserves world-class healthcare, regardless of their background or income level.

What started as a 20-bed clinic in Surulere has grown into a network of three full-service hospitals across Lagos and Abuja, serving over 12,000 patients annually with more than 48 specialist consultants.

We've pioneered minimally invasive surgery in Nigeria, established the first dedicated stroke unit in private healthcare in Lagos, and launched free annual screening camps that have reached over 50,000 underserved Nigerians.

🎯 Our Mission To deliver compassionate, evidence-based healthcare that transforms lives.
👁 Our Vision A Nigeria where quality healthcare is the norm, not the privilege.
🛡 Integrity Transparent, honest practice in everything we do.
🌱 Innovation Continuously adopting the latest medical advances for better outcomes.
