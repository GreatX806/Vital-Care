Flagship Hospital

VitalCare Victoria Island

Our flagship 200-bed hospital on Ozumba Mbadiwe Avenue is the most comprehensive private medical facility in Lagos. Home to our Cardiac Centre, dedicated Stroke Unit, Cancer Care Centre, and robotic-assisted Surgery Suite — all under one roof.

Fully accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI) and ISO 9001 certified, this facility sets the benchmark for private healthcare in Nigeria.

Est. 2012 Lekki Hub
Lekki · Lagos

VitalCare Lekki Phase 1

Strategically positioned to serve the rapidly growing Lekki corridor, our 140-bed Lekki facility offers the full range of outpatient specialties plus a high-dependency unit, modern maternity suite, and a diagnostic imaging centre with 128-slice CT and 1.5T MRI.

The Lekki hospital is also home to our flagship Fertility and IVF Centre, consistently ranked among the top three fertility programmes in Nigeria.

VitalCare Abuja

Our newest and most technologically advanced facility, VitalCare Abuja brings the group's signature standard of care to the Federal Capital Territory. With 80 beds and a focus on outpatient specialist clinics, day-case surgery, and executive health screenings, it is the preferred medical partner for Abuja's diplomatic and corporate community.
